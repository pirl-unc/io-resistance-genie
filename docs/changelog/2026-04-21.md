## Novel findings

### Antigen presentation

- **deltaHED challenges the TMB-centric view of PD-1 biomarkers.** Across POLARIS-02, melanoma, and JUPITER-06 cohorts, high HLA-I evolutionary divergence between germline and somatic alleles predicted *worse* PD-1 outcomes despite higher TMB/neoantigen load. [PMID 41601354](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41601354/).

### IFN-γ signaling

- **IFN-γ–IRF1–AGPAT3 sensitizes tumors to ferroptosis.** IRF1 upregulates AGPAT3, which remodels the lipidome toward polyunsaturated ether phospholipids. AGPAT3 loss impaired IFN-γ-mediated tumor elimination; AGPAT3 expression predicted better ICI survival. Extends the canonical IFN-γ axis into lipid metabolism. [PMID 41807033](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41807033/).

### TME exclusion

- **Six spatial niches stratify neoadjuvant PD-1/PD-L1 response in cSCC.** Single-cell spatial transcriptomics across three cohorts defined niches that outperformed PD-L1 IHC for predicting pathologic response. [PMID 41617396](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41617396/).
- **hMENA TGF-β CAF signature validated in OAK phase III.** Elevates a CAF-centric exclusion mechanism from preclinical biology to a clinical biomarker. [PMID 41592891](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41592891/).
- **TROP2-claudin-7 tight junctions exclude T cells in TNBC.** Reframes TROP2 from pure ADC target to functional exclusion driver; rationalizes TROP2 ADC + anti-PD-1 beyond cytotoxic payload delivery. [PMID 41932810](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41932810/).
- **LOAd703 + atezolizumab restores ICI-responsive signatures in anti-PD-1-refractory melanoma.** 24-patient phase I/II; myeloid reprogramming evidence in a confirmed-refractory population. [PMID 41888981](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41888981/).

### Tumor-intrinsic signaling

- **HILPDA → KLF5 → PD-L1 palmitoylation (Cys272).** Lipid-droplet regulator stabilizes PD-L1 via a PTM; fenretinide engages TRIM21 to degrade HILPDA and restore anti-PD-1 efficacy. [PMID 41876831](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41876831/).
- **Lactate → AARS1 → PD-L1 K280 lactylation blocks HUWE1 ubiquitination.** Links the Warburg axis directly to PD-L1 protein stability. Paradoxical finding: exogenous lactate enhanced anti-PD-L1 efficacy in preclinical models — to be tracked for resolution. [PMID 41864972](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41864972/).
- **NOTCH3 → RBPJ → PVR → TIGIT in CRC.** Tumor-intrinsic driver couples directly to the PD-1/TIGIT axis; low/mutant NOTCH3 predicts better ICB survival in 102-patient + MSKCC cohorts. [PMID 41808828](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41808828/).
- **PKMYT1 inhibition activates cGAS-STING in castration-resistant prostate cancer.** Clinical-grade RP-6306 converts immune-cold CRPC to anti-PD-L1-responsive disease in preclinical models. [PMID 41617394](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41617394/).

### T-cell exhaustion

- **PD-1 protects clonally expanding T cells from restimulation-induced cell death.** *In vitro* human primary T cell study that, if replicated *in vivo*, would invert part of the release-the-brakes framing of PD-1 blockade. High-priority validation target. [PMID 41748562](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41748562/).
- **KLRG1 is a novel inhibitory checkpoint.** Upregulated after checkpoint therapy; KLRG1-high TILs enriched in anti-PD-1-resistant melanoma. A novel anti-human KLRG1 mAb reduces tumor progression in humanized KI mice via CD8, NK, and gd-T effects. [PMID 41956544](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41956544/).
- **T cell-intrinsic VISTA enforces CD8 dysfunction and synergizes with anti-CTLA-4.** Distinct from myeloid VISTA; VISTA-deficient cytotoxic signature correlates with ICI outcomes. [PMID 41837284](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41837284/).
- **M2 TAM-PGE2 drives TIGIT+ terminal exhaustion in MSS CRC.** Specific combinatorial rationale (COX2i or TIGIT blockade + PD-L1) for an indication where PD-1 monotherapy has consistently failed. [PMID 41196020](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41196020/).
- **Anti-TIM-3 (TQB2618) + penpulimab: 52% ORR in PD-1-pretreated cHL.** 21 evaluable patients in phase Ib; meaningful salvage signal in a setting where re-engaging checkpoint biology is not typically expected to work. [PMID 41963080](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41963080/).

### Metabolic

- **Hypoalbuminemia causally drives ICI resistance via macrophage arginine metabolism.** TAM depletion or dietary arginine supplementation rescues anti-PD-1 efficacy in LLC-bearing mice. Reframes a common prognostic biomarker as a mechanism-anchored, reversible driver. [PMID 41940988](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41940988/).

### Clinical interventions

- **ATOMIC (NEJM phase 3): adjuvant atezolizumab + mFOLFOX6 in stage III dMMR colon cancer.** HR 0.50 (95% CI 0.35–0.73), 3-year DFS 86.3% vs 76.2%. Extends ICI benefit to the adjuvant dMMR setting. [PMID 41880612](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41880612/).
- **8-gene k-TSP + mucinous biomarker stratifies anti-CTLA-4 addition benefit in dMMR/MSI-H mCRC.** Biomarker-positive subgroup (n=25, ~15%): 24-month PFS 72.2% with combo vs 13.8% with mono (HR 0.10). Retrospective across non-randomized regimens; prospective validation needed. [PMID 41950572](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41950572/).
